xhouse

An experimental living experience

What

Are you tired of looking for random roommates on the Housing page? We are too. Why not join a community instead! If you're really curious and passionate about things, come join our housing search. We're trying to create a living environment that's creative, positive, and thought-provoking. If you think that's you let us know. We're open to all. Whether you're an engineer or an artist or a philosopher or a scientist, a sophomore tired of the dorms or a senior who wants to try something new. Whether you're from across the world or you're a Bay Area native. The only criteria is that you must love knowledge for knowledge's sake.

Join

If you want to live with a group of people who are curious and passionate, let's look for a place together! Email us your answers for these below, and we'll invite you to our housing search.

  • What do you consider yourself (sort of) an expert at?
  • Do you need a place for the semester or the school year?
  • What three words would you use to describe yourself?
  • Are you okay with roommates or would you prefer a single room?
