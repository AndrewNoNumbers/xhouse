What

Are you tired of looking for roommates on the Housing page? We are too. Join a community instead! If you're really curious and passionate about things, come join our housing search. We're trying to create a living environment that is creative, motivating, and thought-provoking. If that sounds like what you're looking for, let us know. Whether you're an engineer, philosopher, artist, or scientist... a sophomore tired of the Units or a senior wanting to try something new... whether you're from the Bay or the other side of the world... we're open to all. The only criterion is that you must love knowledge for knowledge's sake.

Join
